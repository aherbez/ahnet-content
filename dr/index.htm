This is the first of (hopefully) a series of popup papercraft kits. This is the image that I'll be building into the mechanism:



I've just finished off the design and construction (via Maya, Pepakura Designer, and Illustrator) of the first pass of the kit, with the following results:



There's a pull tab on the bottom of the box (outside the shot). Pulling on that causes the level of the bot's drink to lower, and a mechanical broken heart to rise behind him.
